Overview


  • NIRF Ranking: The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is an initiative by the Government of India to rank higher education institutions. AKTU typically participates in this ranking, where it is assessed across various parameters such as teaching, learning & resources, research & professional practice, graduation outcomes, outreach & inclusivity, and perception.

  • QS World University Rankings: The QS World University Rankings evaluate universities worldwide based on academic reputation, employer reputation, faculty/student ratio, citations per faculty, international faculty ratio, and international student ratio. AKTU's performance in these rankings depends on its global recognition and research impact.

  • Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings: THE World University Rankings assess universities globally on criteria such as teaching, research, citations, international outlook, and industry income. AKTU's standing in these rankings reflects its academic and research excellence on a global scale.

  • Regional Rankings: Some rankings focus on specific regions or countries. AKTU might appear in rankings tailored to the Indian subcontinent or specific states within India, reflecting its performance within those contexts.

  • Subject-specific Rankings: AKTU's programs might also be evaluated in subject-specific rankings. These rankings assess the performance of universities in particular fields such as engineering, technology, management, etc.

  • Employability Rankings: Some rankings assess universities based on the employability of their graduates. AKTU's placement records and industry connections play a significant role in determining its position in such rankings.

  • Faculty Qualifications: Rankings may consider the qualifications and expertise of AKTU's faculty members, including their academic backgrounds, research experience, and industry connections. Highly qualified faculty can contribute to the university's reputation and research output.

  • Infrastructure and Facilities: The availability of state-of-the-art infrastructure, laboratories, libraries, and other facilities can positively impact AKTU's ranking. Modern facilities support quality education, research endeavors, and student learning experiences.

  • Student Satisfaction: Rankings might incorporate measures of student satisfaction, such as student feedback surveys, alumni reviews, and retention rates. Positive feedback from students and alumni can enhance AKTU's reputation and ranking.
